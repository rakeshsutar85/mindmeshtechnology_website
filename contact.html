FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can you start on my project?

We typically onboard new projects within 1–2 weeks of contract signing, depending on team availability and project scope.

Do you offer fixed-price or time-and-material engagements?

Both! We offer fixed-price for well-defined projects and time-and-material for evolving scopes. We'll recommend the best fit during our discovery call.

Can I try your products before buying?

Absolutely. We offer free 14-day trials for all our SaaS products along with a guided onboarding session. Book a demo through this page.

Do you provide post-launch support?

Yes — all our project packages include 60 days of free bug-fix support after launch. Extended SLA-backed maintenance plans are also available.
